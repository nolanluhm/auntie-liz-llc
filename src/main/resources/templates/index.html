About Auntie Liz, LLC

Auntie Liz, LLC is a small business that offers professional housekeeping and pet sitting services to clients in St. Louis and the surrounding areas. After contacting me with an appointment request, you will be assigned a housekeeper and we will finalize your appointment day and time. When your housekeeper arrives, they will start by cleaning all of your windows. Then they will dust, disinfect, and wipe down surfaces in each room. For bathrooms, your housekeeper will work their way from top to bottom and end with cleaning the toilet. Floors (whether hardwood, tile, linoleum, or carpeting) will be completed last. Those that opt for emptying waste cans will have this service completed from room-to-room. All cleaning supplies and equipment will be provided by Auntie Liz, LLC unless otherwise requested. Pet sitting details will be worked out with the client beforehand because every pet is different!

Ecological and Community Initiatives

Auntie Liz, LLC is committed to reducing the harmful effects of landfills and pollution by applying environmentally conscious practices to everyday operations. I recycle all product containers after they are used, wash cleaning rags for reuse, turn off lights after cleaning each space, drive a gas-efficient vehicle, and conduct business primarily online to save paper, to name a few. I find that being as "green" as I can be, both professionally and personally, is one way I can be socially responsible.

I am also committed to giving back to others. Many of my clients know my personal story--two chronically ill parents and several personal health conditions. It is important to me that I use my business to make a difference for personal fulfillment. I can make a difference in individual lives by providing the helpful services that Auntie Liz, LLC has to offer, which benefits each of my clients in various ways. I also offer an array of discounts to clients that fit certain criteria: veterans, elderly, nursing home employees, etc. Every one of my clients receives their fifth cleaning complimentary since I greatly value their patronage and support. I expand this to communities by contributing a portion of my weekly earnings toward various charities of my choice. So far, Auntie Liz, LLC has donated to charities such as: St. Jude's, Alzheimer's Association, Multiple Sclerosis Society, Animal Rescue Foundation of Missouri, Canines in Crisis, Not One More Vet, Active Minds, Breast Cancer Foundation, etc. If my clients wish to select a specific charity in which they want to see supported, they can absolutely request one. I am always looking for new ways to give back, so new ideas are always welcome!
